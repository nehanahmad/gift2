About the Founder

Annadatha Venkata Sai "Don" Lasya was a world renowned Godfather, known as "Don Lasya" through the streets of Bangalore, she was known for her shrewd and disciplined actvities. A skilled lawyer, she could defend any client any client given to her. A woman feared by many...


Because of this she slowly gained influence in the criminal, slowly becoming the Head of the crime syndicate. People refer to her as the "Heavy Don" or "The Gardenia Leader". She is known to have many gangs under her wing, the "Elite Rockerz", "Podar Side" and "Soi Gundas".

But after seeing all this power, "Don Lasya" had a main character moment and she had a redemption arc, she said "I have no enemies" and founded Lassiflix, to help the people of Bangalore.

"Don Lasya" was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize after she ended the gang war between 2 of the most notorious gangs of Bangalore, Southside and South End Circle.


After all these heavy scenes, Lasya went into hiding, it is unknown where she is, but people say she supports the people from the shadows like a true superhero.
